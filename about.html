About Sunrise Chicken Farm

We are a modern poultry farm located in Kenya, focused on raising healthy broilers and producing high-quality eggs for individuals, businesses, and institutions. We believe in sustainable farming practices, animal welfare, and delivering excellence every day.

Experienced Team

Our dedicated staff brings years of expertise in poultry care, nutrition, and modern farming methods.

Clean & Hygienic

We maintain a clean, biosecure environment to ensure our chickens stay healthy and stress-free.

Customer First

Whether you're buying one tray or a thousand, we prioritize your satisfaction with consistent quality.

Our Mission

To provide the community with healthy, fresh, and ethically raised poultry products that meet the highest standards.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of sustainable and trusted poultry farming solutions in Kenya and beyond.
